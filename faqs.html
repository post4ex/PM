Find quick answers to common queries.

You can track your shipment by entering your AWB (Air Waybill) or Reference Number in the tracking widget located on the home page. Our real-time tracking system provides updates on the current location and status of your parcel.

Delays can occur due to weather, customs clearance, or technical issues. If your package is significantly delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, please so our team can investigate immediately.
