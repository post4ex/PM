Our Story

Founded in 2022, Postman began as a modern logistics platform with a simple mission: to connect people and businesses across India through reliable logistics solutions.

In just two years, we've established partnerships with leading courier companies and built a comprehensive network that handles everything from documents to e-commerce packages, international shipments, and specialized cargo.

Today, we continue to innovate with technology-driven solutions while maintaining our commitment to personal service and reliability that our customers have come to expect.
